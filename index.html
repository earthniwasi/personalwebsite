About Me

A bit more about who I am

I'm Kiroj Bhandari, from Urlabari-7, Baghkhor. Right now, most of my structured hours go into studying Chartered Accountancy — a field that has taught me more about discipline and delayed gratification than I expected, and one that I take seriously even when I joke about it.

Outside of that, I live a fairly active and slightly unpredictable life. I play football regularly, train in kickboxing, and treat both as much as a mental reset as physical exercise. I also dabble in trading stocks, which has taught me that markets have zero interest in my study schedule.

Travel and adventure are the parts of my life that keep everything else in perspective — new places, new roads, and the occasional questionable decision along the way. And through all of it, sarcasm remains my default setting; it's less a personality flaw and more a coping mechanism I've fully committed to.
